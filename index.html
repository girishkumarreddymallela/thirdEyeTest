200+ Ways to Fund Your Idea

Navigate institutional, alternative, and non-dilutive government capital networks to back your startup.

Bootstrapping & Grants

  • Govt Schemes & Grants India

    Access non-dilutive capital pools like Startup India Seed Fund, BIRAC Biotechnology Grants, and TIDE 2.0 center schemes.

  • Low-Interest MSME Loans Govt

    Secure collateral-free funding packages under the SIDBI venture capital fund and credit guarantee loan programs (CGTMSE).

  • MUDRA Schemes Micro

    Micro-loans starting from ₹50,000 up to ₹10 Lakhs designed to cover small manufacturing equipment or local office inventory setups.

Angel & VC Funding

  • Vetted Angel Syndicates

    Pitch seed networks and angel groupings looking for convertible notes, SAFEs, and early pre-seed deals ($25K-$150K checks).

  • Institutional Seed Funds

    Connect with micro-VCs and early-stage institutions looking for 10-20% equity stake rounds for SaaS and DeepTech ventures.

  • Investor Database Access

    Get direct access to spreadsheets of 500+ active VC contacts, cap table estimators, and template blueprint slide structures.

Alternative Funding

  • Crowdfunding Campaigns

    Engage direct consumer pre-orders using Kickstarter or Indiegogo platforms to validate product manufacturing tooling costs.

  • Revenue-Based Finance

    Fund marketing and recurring SaaS expenses through non-dilutive debt models that are repaid dynamically as you grow.

  • Accelerators & Alliances

    Access capital, mentors, and corporate pilot programs via tech hub sandboxes and co-development ventures.
